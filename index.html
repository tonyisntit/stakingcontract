Game Fundamentals:

Game Type: One-on-One.

Player 1 Action: Player 1 joins, specifies the number of gems to bet.

Player 2 Joins: Player 2 decides whether to accept the bet.

Bet Acceptance: If Player 2 accepts the bet, an equal amount of gems is immediately deducted from their balance.

Countdown: A 3-second countdown begins, followed by an animation to determine the winner.

Winning Scenario: All the gems bet are awarded to the winner.

Draw Scenario: There is a possibility of a draw, in which case each player receives their bet back minus a 1% fee.

Game Math:

Win Probability: Each player has a 49% chance of winning.

Draw Probability: There is a 2% chance of a draw.
